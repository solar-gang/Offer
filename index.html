2) Proof using bills: Utility vs Solar Net Billing

Solar helps, but Net Billing can still leave you exposed at night. This is why storage makes the biggest difference.

Utility (red) Net Billing Storage impact

Utility-only (problem)

  • High exposure to TOU peaks
  • Rate increases compound over time
  • No backup power during outages

Solar Net Billing (reality)

  • Solar offsets daytime usage
  • Evening power is still expensive
  • Storage is what “wins the night”
